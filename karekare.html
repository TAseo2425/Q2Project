Kare-Kare

Good for six people

Ingredients

  1. 3 lbs oxtail/tripe/beef(sliced, 2in)
  2. 1 sliced small banana flower bud
  3. 1 bundle pechay or bok choy
  4. 1 bundle string beans(sliced, 2in)
  5. 4 sliced eggplants
  6. 1 cup ground peanuts
  7. 1/2 cup peanut butter
  8. 1/2 cup shrimp paste
  9. 34 ounces water
  10. 1/2 cup annatto seeds(soaked in water)
  11. 1/2 cup toasted ground rice
  12. 1 tbsp minced garlic
  13. 1 chopped onion
  14. salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil
  2. Put in the oxtail followed by the onions
  3. Simmer for 2.5 to 3 hrs or until tender
  4. Once the meat is tender, add the ground peanuts, peanut butter, and water from annato mixture
  5. Simmer for 5 to 7 minutes
  6. Add the toasted ground rice
  7. Simmer for 5 minutes
  8. On a separate pan, saute the garlic then add the banana flower, eggplant, and string beans
  9. Cook for 5 minutes
  10. Transfer the cooked vegetables to rest of ingredients
  11. Add pepper and salt
  12. Serve hot
