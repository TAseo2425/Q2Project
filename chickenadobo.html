Chicken Adobo

(Good for four)

Ingredients

  1. 2 lbs chicken (slice into serving pieces)
  2. 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  3. 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  4. 6 tablespoons white vinegar
  5. 5 cloves garlic
  6. 1 1/2 cups water
  7. 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  8. 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  9. 1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
  10. 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Mix well.
  2. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour. (Longer the time, the better)
  3. Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil.
  4. Pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.
  5. Pour-in the remaining marinade, including garlic.
  6. Add water and bring to a boil
  7. Add dried bay leaves and peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes/until the chicken tenders
  8. Add vinegar then stir and cook for 10 minutes.
  9. Put-in the sugar, and salt. Stir. (optional)
  10. Turn the heat off and Serve.
