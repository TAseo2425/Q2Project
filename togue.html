Lumpiang Togue

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb mung bean sprouts
  2. 1 julienned carrot
  3. 4 cloves chopped garlic
  4. 1 sliced onion
  5. 1/4 cup dried shrimp
  6. 8 oz diced fried tofu
  7. 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  8. 2 tbsp fish sauce
  9. 15 pieces lumpia wrapper
  10. 2 cups cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat a wok/frying pan and put-in 2 tbsp of cooking oil
  2. When hot enough, saute garlic and onions
  3. Add the dried shrimp then cook for a minute
  4. Put-in the fried tofu then stir
  5. Add the fish sauce and ground black pepper
  6. Put-in the togue and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Add the carrot and cook for 1 to 2 minutes
  8. Turn off heat or remove from pan before wrapping in lumpia wrapper
  9. Pour remaining oil to deep fry
  10. Deep fry wrapped lumpia until golden brown
  11. After frying, let some paper towels absorb excess oil
  12. Serve hot
