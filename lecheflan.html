Leche Flan

Ingredients

  1. 10 egg yolks
  2. 1 can condensed milk (14oz)
  3. 1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  4. 1 cup granulated sugar
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. Llanera or any mold
  7. aluminum foil

Instructions

  1. Place yolks in a bowl and beat them.
  2. Mix in condensed milk.
  3. Mix in fresh/evaporated milk and vanilla extract
  4. Put mold on stove using low heat.
  5. Put sugar in mold and mix until it liquifies and spread.
  6. Wait 5 minutes before pouring yolk and milk mixture in mold.
  7. Cover mold in aluminum foil before steaming for 30-35 minutes
  8. Cool down and then refrigerate
  9. Serve as dessert
