Sinigang

Good for four people

Ingredients

  1. 2 lbs pork belly
  2. 1 lb young tamarind
  3. 1 bunch chopped water spinach
  4. 8 string beans(cut into 2in)
  5. 2 sliced eggplants
  6. 1 daikon radish
  7. 8 okras
  8. 2 tomatoes(sliced into wedges)
  9. 2 long green pepper
  10. 1 onion(sliced into wedges)
  11. 2 quarts water
  12. Fish sauce and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the young tamarind in 2 quarts of water for 40 minutes. Filter, Squeeze.
  2. Pour the tamarind broth into a cooking pot, Let boil
  3. Add the onion, pork belly, and half of the tomatoes
  4. Skim-off the floating scums, pour 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  5. Cover and continue to simmer for 1 hour
  6. Add daikon radish and eggplants, cook for 5 minutes
  7. Add the long green pepper, string beans, remaining tomatoes, and okra
  8. Cook for 3 minutes
  9. Add the chopped water spinach stalks and season with fish sauce and ground black pepper
  10. Cook for 2 minutes
  11. Put the water spinach leaves
  12. Cover and turn the heat off, let the residual heat cook the leaves for 3 minutes
  13. Serve
