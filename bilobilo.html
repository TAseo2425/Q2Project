Ginataang Bilo-Bilo

Good for six people

Ingredients

  1. 20 glutinous rice balls
  2. 20 oz sliced ripe jackfruit
  3. 2 cups water
  4. 2 cups coconut cream
  5. 3/4 cup granulated white sugar
  6. 1 1/2 cups cooked tapioca pearls

Instructions

  1. Pour water in cooking pot and bring to a boil
  2. Stir coconut cream in and cook until it boils again
  3. Gradually stir-in the sugar
  4. Add the ripe jackfruit
  5. Cover and cook in low heat for 15 minutes
  6. Add-in the bilo-bilo
  7. Cook for another 15 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes
  8. Put-in sago
  9. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes
  10. Serve
